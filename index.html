Why Lunch Matters?

Choosing the right lunch is crucial for maintaining energy levels throughout the day. A balanced meal can boost your productivity and mood.

Tips for a Healthy Lunch:

  • Include Protein: Chicken, fish, tofu, or beans help keep you full.
  • Add Vegetables: Aim for a variety of colors to get different nutrients.
  • Hydrate: Don't forget to drink water with your meal.
  • Mindful Eating: Take time to enjoy your food away from screens.
